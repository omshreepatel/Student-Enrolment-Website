SEMESTER ENROLMENT WEBSITE

This website is based on student's semester registration in college. This site takes information of students and help the department members to keep the track of students database. Registration done in paper for new semester is a monotonous and hectic process for students as well as for staff member. If any mistake occurs in hand filled then it cannot be tracked easily. Not only students, staff also faces problem in managing data (enrolment no., name, result, etc.) of large number of students. This site will help students in registration in online mode for next semester and maintenance of records by staff members. The main objective of this site is to provide easy and comfortable registration process.
